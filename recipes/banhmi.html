Bánh Mì Heo Quay (Vietnamese Roast Pork Baguette)

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette (Vietnamese-style or French-style)
  • 200g roast pork belly (heo quay), sliced thinly
  • Pickled carrots and daikon (đồ chua)
  • Fresh cucumber slices
  • Fresh cilantro (ngò rí)
  • Mayonnaise
  • Maggi seasoning sauce or soy sauce
  • Optional: sliced chili or jalapeño for heat
  • Butter (optional, for toasting the bread)

Steps

  1. Slice the baguette lengthwise but not all the way through, keeping it like a hinge.
  2. Lightly toast the baguette, optionally spreading butter before toasting for extra flavor.
  3. Spread mayonnaise evenly on the inside of the bread.
  4. Layer the sliced roast pork belly inside the baguette.
  5. Add pickled carrots and daikon, cucumber slices, and fresh cilantro on top.
  6. Drizzle a little Maggi seasoning sauce or soy sauce over the filling.
  7. Add sliced chili if you want some spiciness.
  8. Close the baguette gently and serve immediately.
