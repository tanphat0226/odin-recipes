Bánh Tráng Muối Ớt (Vietnamese Spicy Salted Rice Paper Snack)

Ingredients

  • 3-4 sheets of rice paper (bánh tráng)
  • 1 tablespoon chili salt (muối ớt)
  • 1 tablespoon dried shrimp (tôm khô), finely ground (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil or scallion oil (dầu hành)
  • 1 tablespoon fried shallots
  • 1 teaspoon lime juice or kumquat juice
  • Fresh herbs (e.g., Vietnamese coriander - rau răm), finely chopped
  • Quail eggs or boiled eggs (optional)
  • Mayonnaise and chili sauce (for topping)

Steps

  1. Cut rice paper into bite-sized strips or squares using scissors.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine chili salt, sugar, dried shrimp (if using), and a bit of lime or kumquat juice.
  3. Add the rice paper into the bowl and gently toss to coat evenly.
  4. Drizzle scallion oil over the mixture for fragrance and moisture.
  5. Mix in fried shallots and chopped herbs.
  6. If using, slice boiled quail eggs and mix them in gently.
  7. Top with mayonnaise and chili sauce to taste.
  8. Serve immediately to enjoy the chewy, spicy, and savory flavor.
