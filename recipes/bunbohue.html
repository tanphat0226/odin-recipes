Bún Bò Huế (Spicy Vietnamese Beef Noodle Soup)

Ingredients

  • 500g beef shank or brisket
  • 300g pork hock or pork knuckles
  • 200g beef bones (for broth)
  • 300g bún (thick round rice noodles)
  • 2 stalks lemongrass, bruised and tied
  • 1 onion, peeled and halved
  • 3-4 dried shrimp
  • 2 tablespoons shrimp paste (mắm ruốc)
  • 3 tablespoons chili oil
  • 1 tablespoon annatto oil (dầu điều) or oil infused with paprika
  • Fish sauce to taste
  • Salt and sugar to taste
  • Fresh herbs: cilantro, Vietnamese mint (rau răm), green onions
  • Shredded banana blossoms (optional)
  • Lime wedges
  • Thinly sliced red onions and chili slices

Steps

  1. Clean and blanch beef shank, pork hock, and bones in boiling water for a few minutes to remove impurities. Rinse and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, add bones, beef shank, pork hock, lemongrass, onion, dried shrimp, and enough water to cover. Simmer for 2-3 hours until broth is rich.
  3. Remove meat and bones. Slice beef shank thinly. Shred pork hock meat if desired.
  4. In a small pan, heat annatto oil or chili oil until fragrant. Add shrimp paste and stir to combine.
  5. Add shrimp paste mixture into the broth, stir well. Season broth with fish sauce, salt, and sugar to taste.
  6. Cook the bún noodles according to package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  7. To serve: place noodles in bowls, top with sliced beef, shredded pork, herbs, banana blossoms, onions, and chili slices.
  8. Ladle hot broth over the bowl and serve with lime wedges on the side.
